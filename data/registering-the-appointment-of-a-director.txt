Following incorporation, you must register any new director within 20 working days of their appointment.Time limit for completing registrationAfter you've entered a director's details on the Companies Register, we email you a consent form that must be signed by the director and returned to us within 20 working days.Important        We send a series of short emails reminding you to return the consent form. If it isn't received within 20 working days, all information about the director is deleted from the Companies Register and you must begin the registration process again.    Information you need to complete registrationTo register the appointment of a director you must tell us their:full legal namedate and place of birth — this information is not made publicly availableresidential address — this must be a physical street address, not a PO Box, Private Bag or DX servicedate of appointment.You can also tell us their email address, telephone, mobile and fax numbers if you want.Note        Sometimes we request additional information to confirm the identity or personal information of a proposed director.    When you enter a director's details, our system automatically alerts you if this person is listed as a banned director or undischarged bankrupt. A warning also appears if this person has a similar name to a banned person                                                                                 Banned                                            directors                                Authority to register a directorTo register a new director, you must have:a RealMe® loginan online services account with the Companies Registerauthority to act for your company. Add the director's detailsLog in to your online services account, enter a company name, company number or New Zealand Business Number (NZBN) and follow these steps.Select the Directors tab.Select Add new director.Enter all requested information.Select Submit.                    Update director details                                2. Sign and return the consent formOnce you've entered the director's details, we email you a consent form in that person's name.The director must sign and return the form to us within 20 working days. In signing the form, the director certifies they're not disqualified from being appointed, or holding office as a director.
