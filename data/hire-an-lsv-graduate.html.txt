Volunteer graduate. Build your team with young people from your community, ready to work and succeed.As an employer you’ll have the opportunity to shape the future of young members of your community.You’ll provide the chance for these young people to build a better future and become a valued member of your team.Information“LSV graduates are quick to learn and adapt to their new roles. And come with a strong desire to work and succeed.” Auckland DHB, Santosh ParabWhat is LSVLimited Service Volunteer (LSV) is a 6-week training course for 18-24 year olds. It gives them confidence, life skills and a willingness to learn and grow in their future workplace. LSV is a live-in course run in partnership with New Zealand Defence Force. Information“LSV is a win-win. We help young people into employment, knowing the graduates we take on have self-discipline, determination and stamina.” Fulton Hogan, NZ CEO Graeme JohnsonMore information about LSVWhy employ a LSV graduateLSV graduates:are motivatedare self-disciplinedvalue teamwork, andhave respect for others. Young people volunteer to take part in LSV. They will learn new skills and get to know more about who they are, what they want to do and what it takes to get there.They're ready to work, learn and have the determination to make a positive contribution to your business.InformationLSV graduates make stellar employees and go on to be amazing role models and invaluable community leaders.SuperTyre, Shane EpihaMeet the LSV traineesCome to an LSV courseTalk to us about coming to an LSV course in Auckland, Wellington or Christchurch. You'll meet our young people and see first-hand what motivates them to get a job and succeed.We can:search for suitable candidates who would be a good fit for your businessshortlist candidates for you to considerarrange times for you to interview the candidateswe may also be able to provide a space for you to hold an interview, if you need it.  connect you with information and services if you need advice: before making an employment offer, eg, we can help complete reference checksabout making an employment offer.   Take part in a LSV employer expoTalk to us about taking part in an employer expo. You’ll meet trainees and can recruit a LSV graduate directly from the LSV course.By attending, you can hear more about what skills the trainees have gained and about their interests.
