usThe NZ Product Accelerator is a network of industry-minded researchers across New Zealand who are driven by focusing on the missing science that can make the difference for your business’ growth. Whether that requires making connections to other industry partners, setting up initial testing or exploring speculative research projects, the NZ Product Accelerator is worth having a conversation to.  Benefits to your businessFill 1 Copy 2Best team approach and network effectsGet connected to the combined expertise from across the entire country.Fill 1 Copy 2Easy accessAccessible costs, a collaborative mindset and flexible IP policy make the engagement easy.Fill 1 Copy 2Different thinkingCreate new momentum and think differently about your problems and opportunities.Is this right for me?Best suited toAny business that deals with manufactured products through design, materials or processing. FundingThe NZPA network is funded by The Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment to have initial conversations with industry and to identify opportunities for further support.  Subsequent project work generally requires the customer paying an agreed fee-for-service. More details can be found on the NZPA website. Get involvedDoes something you're working on need additional scientific support?
