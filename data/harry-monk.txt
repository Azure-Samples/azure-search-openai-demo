specialising in Advanced Engineering and Materials. This uses my expertise in particle size analysis, chemical analysis, and cellulose polymers to contribute to innovative research projects.During my masters degree in Chemistry from the Ferrier Research Institute, I studied the development of cellulose encapsulated agrichemical formulations. This experience equipped me with a deep understanding of materials and their applications, instilling a strong foundation in the field of materials science.In my previous role as a QC Chemistry Technician at MSD Animal Health, I had the privilege of running a Quality Control chemistry lab.
