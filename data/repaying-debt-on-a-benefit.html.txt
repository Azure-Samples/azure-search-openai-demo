not on benefit or pensionChildcareHaving another childArrest warrantsYour rights and responsibilitiesRepaying debt while getting a benefit or pensionIf you're getting a main benefit, NZ Super, Veteran's Pension or Accommodation Supplement, we'll work with you on a repayment plan that suits you. You can also set up a separate repayment arrangement if you want to.Go to the page 'Your debt with us' if you want information about:how to get a breakdown of your debtwhat to do if you don't agree with your debtsupport for managing your debt Debt repayment informationIf you're not getting a main benefit, NZ Super, Veteran's Pension or Accommodation Supplement, there's a different process for your debt repayments.What we mean by 'main benefit'Repaying debt when not getting a benefit or pension from usDebt repayment amountWe'll set up a repayment amount depending on your circumstances. We want to make sure this is affordable for you.When deciding how much your repayments should be, you'll need to think of things like:your budgetyour upcoming expensesany other debts you may havehow long you want to be repaying the money. Call us on 0800 559 009 to:discuss debt repaymentsset up a time to talk to your case manager, if you want totalk to us about any questions you have about your debt. Setting up debt repaymentsCall us on 0800 559 009 to set up debt repayments. We'll set these up so they come out of your benefit, pension or Accommodation Supplement. If you want to pay the debt off faster you can make extra repayments at any time.You can also set up a separate repayment arrangement via internet or telephone banking if you want to.If you're getting a Student Allowance, you need to contact StudyLink.Set up repayment via internet or telephone bankingYou can make a one-off payment or set up regular payments using internet or telephone banking. Your bank may charge you for these services.Account numberTo set up the payments you'll need the Work and Income Debtors Account number: 03-0049-0006243-025 (the suffix can be 025 or 25).ReferenceIn the reference field, you need to put the letter 'S', followed by your 9 digit client number.If your benefit, pension or Accommodation Supplement is about to stopIf you owe us money and your benefit, pension or Accommodation Supplement is about to stop, you need to call us on 0800 559 009 to:let us know when your payment needs to stoparrange an exit interview with us where we can discuss your repayment options. You'll be able to pay your debt off either:in a lump sum, oreach week at an amount you can afford. If you're going into full-time workIf you’re going into full-time work and can get Assistance to Transition into Employment, you may be able to stop repaying your debt for 3 months. This is called a 3 month debt recovery suspension. This is to help you pay for work related costs.Talk to us at your exit interview about whether you qualify for this payment.RelatedYour debt with usHow we can helpBenefits and paymentsWorkOn a benefitHousingOnline servicesCheck what you might getFind workApply onlineMyMSDHow to stay safe onlineAboutAbout Work and IncomeAbout this siteAccessibilityPrivacy, disclaimer and copyrightContact usPhone numbers                    Find a service centre                    Facebook                    Twitter                    Feedback                              Help in other languages        Te Kāwanatanga o Aotearoa /
