LaboratoryResource: EU AI Act SupportUnderstanding the impact: Resource for New Zealand Innovators on navigating the EU AI ActView resource contact usIf you’re looking to develop, deploy or use an AI system in the EU, you need to be aware of the AI Act’s requirements and understand how they might apply to you.Prepare for the EU AI ActIn a pivotal moment for global Artificial Intelligence (AI) regulation, the European Union (EU) has unveiled the EU AI Act, a comprehensive legislative framework destined to shape the AI governance landscape by addressing risks linked to the development, commercialisation and use of AI technologies.The latest version of the AI Act is available online through The AI Act Explorer. Recognising the potential challenges and opportunities this presents for New Zealand innovators, we have developed targeted resources to help you prepare for the Act’s implementation and drive trust in your use of AI.  Resource1. How to prepareUnderstand the AI Act, your broader obligations and how to develop your AI governance downloadThese fact sheets summarise the key aspects of the EU AI Act and are not legal advice.
