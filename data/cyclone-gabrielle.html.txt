disabilityLiving expensesYou're not workingRelationshipsSeniorsUrgent costsMoving to NZCyclone GabrielleThere are lots of ways we can help, and we can point you in the right direction based on what you need help with.What do you need help with?AccommodationNowhere to stayIf you've had to evacuate and need accommodation, you can register with the Temporary Accommodation Service. You can do this online on the Temporary Accommodation Service website, or call them on 0508 754 163. They will contact you within 2 working days to understand your needs.Paying for accommodation costsYou may be able to get an Accommodation Supplement. This is a weekly payment to help pay for rent, board or the cost of owning a home.Find out more about Accommodation SupplementUrgent or unexpected costsIf you need help with urgent or unexpected costs due to the cyclone, we may be able to help. We can help paying for things like:foodclothing and beddingrefilling water tanksemptying septic tanksrepairs or replacing appliances. What you qualify for will depend on your situation and you may have to pay the money back.Call us on 0800 559 009 to talk to us about how we can help, or you can visit your local service centre.Urgent or unexpected costsWorkWe can help you with:searching for jobs availabletrainingwriting a CV or cover letternew clothes for job interviewspaying for work equipmentpaying for childcare while you work.
