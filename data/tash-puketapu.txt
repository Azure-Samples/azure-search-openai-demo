LaboratoryTash PuketapuMāori Innovation Strategy LeadMāori InnovationShaping a future of prosperity and equityE nga mana, nga reo, e nga karangatanga ō te motuTihei wa mauri ora. E tu ana te manu i tōku maunga ō Hikurangi, ki runga i te pakihiwi ā Maui tikitiki.Ka titiro whakararo ki tōku awa ō Waiapu e rere kau ana ki te moana nui-a-kiwa. Kei reira tōku waka ō Horouta e tau ana.Ka huri tōku mata ki tōku marae ō Rāhui. Ko te wahi nohia ai e ōku tipuna.Ka rere te manu ki Te Tai Tokerau. I āwhina rātou te manu. I whāngai rātou ki tēnei manu ki raro i te maunga ō Motatau. Kei raro i te korowai ō Ngāti Hine, Te Orewai.Nā Ngāti Hine raua ko Ngāti Porou i whanake ai i tēnei manu. Kei Tāmaki Makaurau ahau e noho ana.Ko Tash Puketapu ahau.  As the Māori Innovation Strategy Lead at Callaghan Innovation, I am committed to leveraging my expertise and experience to drive positive change and enhance the quality of life for our mokopuna.With a background in Talent Acquisition, Strategy Development, and Project Delivery within the Public Sector, I'm driven by the vision of uplifting the economic success of Māori and addressing historical inequities.I believe in the power of business as a vehicle for Māori to create intergenerational wealth, fostering a future where prosperity and equity are not just aspirations but realities. BackAreas of expertiseStrategy, Talent, Project Management and the Māori EconomyEducationTe Ara Reo Māori (Te Wānanga o Aotearoa) Stay informedFirst NameLast NameEmail AddressYou must enable JavaScript to submit this formReceive news, events, and opportunities that could drive innovation in your business.
