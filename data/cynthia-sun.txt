Proteins and Enzymes team within the Biotechnologies group. I have deep experience and expertise in the field of biochemistry around biotransformation, protein extraction, glycoenzymes and bioassays.Working alongside a team of exceptionally talented and smart individuals is a core motivation as we get challenged daily by customer requested projects needing research.
