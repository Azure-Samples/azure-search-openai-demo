LaboratoryRhonda VincentFunding Engagement SpecialistFunding EngagementGuiding collaborations towards actionable solutionsI am a Funding Engagement Specialist at Callaghan Innovation helping align customers with the best funding for their situation and needs. Through my expertise in networking and connecting like-minded individuals, I excel in guiding collaborations which develop and communicate simple actionable solutions from complex situations. I thrive on challenges and embrace variety, maintaining a positive outlook that allows me to view difficulties as opportunities for personal and professional growth. We’re all on a mission to grow 100 word-beating, world-bettering companies.
