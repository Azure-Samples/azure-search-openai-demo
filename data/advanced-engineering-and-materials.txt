LaboratoryAdvanced Engineering and MaterialsWe help you to make engineering decisions and material choices confidently, accelerating your design and development process. Contact Us  View Our Other Capabilities Use our specialised services to solve complex design and manufacturing challenges. We offer materials expertise and analysis in metals, ceramics and cements plus a broad spectrum of computer simulation capabilities to facilitate the development and manufacture of products that perform. Our expertiseMechanical EngineeringOur Mechanical Engineering team can help with everything from prototype development and production line set up, to sustainability redesign to, manufacturing process optimisation, and cryogenics, ensuring comprehensive support for your engineering needs.Modelling and SimulationUsing advanced engineering simulation software, we can help address engineering challenges at every stage of the product development process, including testing the performance of a device to ensure effective design and functionality, simulating system changes, and modelling fluid dynamics. Metals and Advanced CeramicsAccess comprehensive solutions to address your materials-related challenges, including analysing and characterising material properties to investigate performance issues, recommending raw material processing and shaping techniques, exploring 3D printing with metals or advanced ceramics, providing thermal processing capabilities, and assisting in material selection optimisation based on factors like strength, cost, and sustainability.CementsWe can guide you on the use of natural materials for sustainable building products, evaluate environmentally friendly alternatives to Portland cement, find innovative ways to reuse waste products and repurpose building materials,  help you optimise mix designs for 3D printing concrete and provide insights into the cement industry's latest trends and advancements.Material Testing Services Our Material Testing Services encompass a wide array of capabilities, including characterising the properties of powder samples, measuring the failure load of components, and offering expertise and tools for comprehensive material characterisation.
