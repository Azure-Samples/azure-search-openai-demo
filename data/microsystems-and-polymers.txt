challenges across design, materials and textiles, electromechanical and manufacturing aspects of microsystems and components. Notably, our work includes creating solutions for sustainable packaging and innovative textiles derived from repurposed materials.Our expertisePolymersWe specialise in aiding sustainable packaging transitions, offering alternatives for non-recyclable packaging, guiding eco-friendly product development, and performing precise chemical and physical analysis of polymers.TextilesOur team excels in creating innovative textiles using repurposed and natural materials. Our expertise extends to recycling end-of-life textiles, diagnosing performance issues, recommending optimal fibre and material choices, adding specific functionalities like flame resistance, and offering product labelling and testing guidance.UltrasoundWe provide advanced solutions for defect detection and void identification within objects, non-destructive characterisation of mechanical properties for various materials, custom-designed ultrasonic transducers, and support in creating precision-targeted drug delivery systems.SensorsWhether you require optimisation of existing methods or entirely new approaches, we're here to help. Our expertise includes physical and chemical detection methods (including environmental pollutants) and comprehensive material property characterisation.OpticsWe can assist with all of your optics and image processing needs.
