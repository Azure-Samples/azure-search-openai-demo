HomeEmployers  Recruitment services (Link 1)Use our free recruitment service to advertise your vacancies and find the right candidate. We do all the hard work, like advertising the job, shortlisting candidates and organising interviews.Support for new employees (Link 3)You may be able to get a wage contribution or other support to hire someone. Find out about Flexi-wage, Apprenticeship Boost, Mana in Mahi and other employment programmes and support.Redundancy support for employers (Link 1)Our dedicated team can help your business if you're struggling. We may be able to help you retrain and keep your staff on, or support them to find a new job.Employer support for Cyclone Gabrielle in Tairāwhiti and Hawke's Bay (Link 1)There is a range of support available to help you and your business if you've been affected by Cyclone Gabrielle in Tairāwhiti or Hawke's Bay.Flexi-wage subsidy (Link 2)Get help to hire and train staff. You could get help to train employees to take on new jobs for a while, especially if COVID-19 affects your business.Debt deductions (Link 3)If your employee has a debt with us, we may ask you to make deductions from their wages so they can repay it.Help us improve our employment services (Link 4)If you've worked with us recently, we may ask for your feedback. Completing an online survey about your recent experience with us helps us improve what we do and how we do it.Partner with us for your recruitment needs (Link 5)If you're a large business or industry association, become one of our industry partners.
