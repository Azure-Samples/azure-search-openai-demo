We support you all along the way

Our Customer Engagement Specialists are from scientific or technological backgrounds and are experienced in doing R&D.

If you are new to the RDTI, they can support you with your application. This is a free service.

Our Customer Engagement Specialists can meet with you to:

  • help you determine whether your R&D may be eligible
  • discuss how to structure your application
  • provide feedback on your drafts, using the General Approval application template .

Please complete the short questionnaire below. Many of the questions are optional, and are asked in case you would like to discuss other types of support from Callaghan Innovation, in addition to the RDTI.
