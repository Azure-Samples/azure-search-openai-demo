Our Other CapabilitiesTap into our bio-innovation capabilities, including developing and characterising enzymes for specific applications, such as specialty chemicals, functional foods, cosmetics, and more.Our expertiseEnzyme Process DevelopmentOur team has experience in industrial and scaled use of enzymatic reactions to generate new products, and the processing of glycoproteins and lipids. We can engineer biological systems to produce high-value speciality chemicals and provide innovative solutions to your product development challenges.Protein Assay DesignWhether you need assays to measure key properties of proteins or  QC testing to keep consistency from batch to batch, we can develop customised assays for your specific requirements. Our capabilities range from biochemical and functional analysis of small and large macromolecules, to chemical profiling of component fractions, cell culture assays, and natural and processed biomaterials.Recombinant Expression of ProteinsWith specialist expertise and facilities, we are uniquely placed to help life science companies with genetic engineering and recombinant protein expression projects. We can design and synthesise genes and gene constructs with a suite of expression vectors for protein expression in various microbial, yeast, and mammalian cell lines. Our commitment extends beyond the laboratory, ensuring a seamless transition to commercial-scale production or handover to a manufacturer.Antibody GenerationWhether you seek to replicate an existing antibody, create a novel antibody for immunoassays, or target a new protein, we offer a range of antibody technologies for the production, isolation and purification of recombinant antibodies.Mammalian Cell BiologyWe offer a range of mammalian cell biology services for various applications, including producing vaccines, monoclonal antibodies, and other therapeutic proteins. Our experts can help you with all your cell culture needs, and we can measure the bioactivity of your product to assess its performance and functionality.Protein Analysis and CharacterisationOur protein analysis and characterisation services provide the insights you need to advance your research and product development.
