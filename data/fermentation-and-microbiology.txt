team will work with you to design and develop a biomanufacturing process that meets your criteria. You can access our equipment and experts to help with all project stages and all aspects of process development – from proof of concept bench-scale, through to 1,000L pilot scale.Fermentation at ScaleDraw upon our expertise in manufacturing at scale to ensure that your enzyme, protein, or live cell product prototypes can be produced at the necessary volumes for testing and field trials. We help to de-risk the scale-up process for a seamless transition from R&D to production.Food Grade FermentationWe have the expertise and infrastructure to produce high-quality products that meet the rigorous standards for human consumption. If you're developing functional foods, dietary supplements, or other edible products, our services ensure your products are safe for the market.Precision FermentationWhether you aim to develop unique bioactive compounds or enhance the functionality of existing products, our precision fermentation capabilities offer innovative solutions. We maintain Environmental Protection Authority approvals and can process genetically modified organisms (GMOs) from 1L up to 1,000L.Microbiology ServicesWe offer a broad range of microbiology services, including conducting assays to screening antimicrobial compounds from natural products, and providing valuable insights into its potential applications and efficacy.Biological Containment Facilities (PC1/PC2)For projects involving pathogens or toxins, safety is paramount.
